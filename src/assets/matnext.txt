Product and Material Tracking, Traceability and Sustainability Platform

MatNEXT is an end-to-end materials traceability and management platform that enables OEMs and value chain partners to track recycled content, carbon footprint, and regulatory compliance across every stage of production, from scrap to finished vehicle. By digitizing material flows beyond individual suppliers, MatNEXT delivers verified, audit-ready insights that transform compliance into measurable sustainability and operational advantage.
